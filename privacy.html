Privacy Policy

This application accesses only DMB LLC's own QuickBooks Online company data (chart of accounts, journal entries, purchase transactions, and standard financial reports) via the QuickBooks Online API, for the sole purpose of posting accounting entries derived from DMB LLC's own ScrapIT reports. No data is shared with, sold to, or accessible by any third party. Data is not collected from or about any other business or individual. Authentication tokens are stored locally on DMB LLC's own machine and are not transmitted to or stored on any third-party server.
